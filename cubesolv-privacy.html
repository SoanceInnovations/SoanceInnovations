CubeSolv Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

CubeSolv works offline, does not require an account, and does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Information we collect

CubeSolv does not collect personal information, device identifiers, analytics, advertising data, or precise location.

Camera and cube images

If you use Scan & Solve, CubeSolv accesses the camera only after you grant permission. Captured cube-face images are processed locally on your device to detect sticker colours. They are not uploaded or transmitted to us or any third party.

Information stored on your device

Level progress, best move counts, and completion times are stored locally on your device. Removing the app may remove this local information, depending on your device settings.

Sharing and third parties

CubeSolv does not share user data with third parties. The app contains no advertising SDK, analytics SDK, social login, or cloud account service.

Children

CubeSolv is a general-audience puzzle app and is not designed primarily for children under 13. Because the app does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if CubeSolv's features or data practices change. The latest version will show its effective date above.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@soance.com.
