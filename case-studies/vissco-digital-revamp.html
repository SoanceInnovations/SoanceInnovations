Vissco · Consumer healthcare · Client project

Rebuilding Vissco’s Digital Product-Discovery Platform

Reading time about 4 mins
Summary
Overview
Challenges
Solution
Results

Summary / TLDR

Vissco needed to replace an aging website with a modern business and product-information platform. The new experience had to organize more than 1,000 orthopedic solutions and mobility aids while remaining understandable to customers with different needs and levels of product knowledge.

The revamp combined strategy, responsive design, WordPress development, content migration, quality assurance, and ongoing support into one maintainable product-discovery platform.

Overview

Vissco manufactures orthopedic supports, mobility aids, and rehabilitation products. Its website needed to work as a comprehensive information and discovery destination—showing the range clearly, supporting research and educational content, and extending the brand beyond physical stores and traditional distribution.

Services provided

  • Digital strategy and experience design
  • Responsive frontend and WordPress development
  • Product taxonomy, content migration, and SEO continuity
  • Cross-browser, functional, and usability testing
  • Maintenance, security updates, and feature support

Challenges

  • A catalog of 1,000+ products: Categories, specifications, and use cases needed a structure that did not overwhelm visitors.
  • CMS migration without search loss: Content had to move to a new system while protecting established SEO value.
  • Broad audience needs: The same platform had to support consumers, caregivers, professionals, and international visitors.
  • Education as well as discovery: Product pages needed to sit alongside useful resources that build understanding and confidence.

Solution

The redesign began with navigation and user-flow strategy, then established a responsive visual system aligned with the healthcare brand. Product content was restructured around clearer categories and more informative detail pages.

  • Created a scalable taxonomy and search-friendly product-page model.
  • Migrated and optimized existing content with redirects and SEO continuity in mind.
  • Added richer product information, educational resources, and clearer calls to action.
  • Validated forms, browsing, and responsive behavior across devices and browsers.
  • Established a maintenance rhythm for content, security, performance, and enhancements.

Results

The redesigned platform gave Vissco a stronger digital storefront for product education and discovery, with a foundation that could continue to expand alongside the catalog.

  • Wider access to product information beyond physical retail locations
  • Clearer browsing across a large and technically varied catalog
  • More useful educational content around orthopedic care and product selection
  • A maintainable CMS that supports ongoing search, content, and feature work
