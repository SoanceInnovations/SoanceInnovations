Summary / TLDR

CineCurrent is an entertainment newsroom covering film, streaming, interviews, and popular culture. The team needed a high-traffic publishing platform that kept editorial teams fast and readers engaged across devices.

Soance shaped the strategy, experience, and technical foundation around measurable user and operational needs, then translated the concept into a launch-ready digital product.

Overview

CineCurrent is a fictional entertainment media company created for this representative portfolio story. Its operating context, user needs, and delivery constraints are modeled on realistic digital transformation engagements.

The core brief was to deliver a high-traffic publishing platform that kept editorial teams fast and readers engaged across devices while giving internal teams a system they could confidently operate, measure, and evolve after launch.

Services provided

  • Content strategy and information architecture
  • Editorial experience design
  • CMS and front-end development
  • Search, structured data, analytics, and performance

Challenges

The project needed to balance a polished customer experience with the less-visible realities of content, operations, permissions, integrations, and long-term maintainability.

  • Breaking-news and live-update workflows: The experience needed to support this capability without adding unnecessary complexity for users or operational teams.
  • Video, gallery, and interview formats: The experience needed to support this capability without adding unnecessary complexity for users or operational teams.
  • Performance, advertising, and analytics integration: The experience needed to support this capability without adding unnecessary complexity for users or operational teams.

Solution

We organized the content around audience intent, created reusable editorial formats, and delivered a fast publishing platform with strong search, structured metadata, and accessible reading patterns.

  • Designed and validated breaking-news and live-update workflows as part of one coherent, role-aware product journey.
  • Designed and validated video, gallery, and interview formats as part of one coherent, role-aware product journey.
  • Designed and validated performance, advertising, and analytics integration as part of one coherent, role-aware product journey.

A shared design system, instrumentation plan, and staged release approach kept the product coherent while allowing the team to learn from realistic usage patterns.

Illustrative outcomes

Because CineCurrent is fictional, these outcomes describe the intended value of the solution rather than claims about a real client engagement.

  • More discoverable content and stronger topic authority
  • A faster, more consistent editorial workflow
  • Improved reading experience across mobile and desktop
