Jagoinvestor · Financial education · Client project

Building a More Accessible Financial Learning Platform for Jagoinvestor

Reading time about 4 mins
Summary
Overview
Challenges
Solution
Results

Summary / TLDR

Jagoinvestor has helped Indian audiences understand personal finance since 2008. Its digital platform needed to make a deep library of articles, calculators, guides, and learning resources approachable for people with very different levels of financial confidence.

The engagement brought design, development, quality assurance, and ongoing maintenance together around one goal: give readers a dependable place to learn, explore scenarios, and make better-informed financial decisions.

Overview

Jagoinvestor is a personal-finance education platform built to simplify complex money topics and help individuals work toward practical financial goals. The brief was to create a responsive, easy-to-manage website that could support frequent publishing while keeping tools and long-form guidance easy to discover.

Services provided

  • Experience strategy and responsive interface design
  • CMS-based website development and content architecture
  • Financial calculator, resource, and search integration
  • Cross-device quality assurance and performance optimization
  • Security updates, backups, maintenance, and feature support

Challenges

  • Making complex tools feel simple: Financial calculators needed accurate processing without creating an intimidating experience for first-time users.
  • Supporting many devices: Articles and interactive tools had to remain fast and usable across browsers, screen sizes, and connection conditions.
  • Balancing depth with clarity: The platform needed to present detailed financial guidance through an understandable hierarchy and consistent visual language.
  • Staying dependable over time: A high-volume publishing platform requires continuous security, backup, performance, and compatibility work.

Solution

The experience was organized around clear topic paths, prominent search, and reusable content patterns. Responsive layouts kept articles, infographics, and tools readable on mobile, while the CMS gave the editorial team direct control over publishing and updates.

  • Integrated calculators and interactive guidance into the wider learning journey.
  • Used caching, image optimization, and cross-platform testing to protect performance.
  • Established a clear visual hierarchy for articles, topic hubs, and downloadable resources.
  • Added routine monitoring, recovery processes, security patches, and feedback-led improvements.

Results

The completed platform gave readers a clearer route into financial education and gave the Jagoinvestor team a maintainable foundation for ongoing growth.

  • Easier discovery of articles, calculators, and practical planning resources
  • A consistent experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Search-friendly content structures that support wider online reach
  • A flexible CMS and maintenance process ready for new topics and features
